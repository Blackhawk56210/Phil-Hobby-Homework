About my favorite hobby

Gaming

My first introduction to gaming was from my older brother. I remember him playing Donkey Kong on the Gamecube with the little plug-in bongos like an early rock band and some other stuff like DMC 3 and, I think, MGS2. I didn't get my first gaming console until I was, I think, around 8 when the PS3 came out, and that's when it led me to play stuff like Call of Duty and Battlefield. I met a lot of great people around that time and got into some scenarios I shouldn't have.


But when I got into high school, that's when I met my current group of friends, whom I've known for just under a decade and who I still play with today. Currently, we've been banging out ranked on Marvel rivals but I'm still taking out my backlog of single-player stuff when I can. I don't remember when I built my PC ill be honest I rarely use it for gaming since I have such little storage space and my hands are so used to a controller although that is where I played most of the Xbox originals like Halo and Gears. But stuff like Custom Zombies and Lethal Company is always fun. Needless to say, I haven't touched it since.
